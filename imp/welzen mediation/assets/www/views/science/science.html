The Science

Stress Reduction

Research shows that mindfulness meditation decreases the levels of the stress hormone “cortisol”, fortifying a sense of calmness throughout the nervous system and the brain.

Manage Anxiety

Studies indicate that Mindfulness meditation reduces the grey-matter density in areas of the brain related to anxiety, concluding to be as effective as antidepressants.

Improve Focus

Studies have shown that Mindfulness meditation practitioners have significantly better ability to focus attention and suppress distracting information.

Increase Creativity

Cognitive Psychology studies have demonstrated that Mindfulness meditation develops divergent thinking, a primary driver of creativity and innovation.

Improved Relationships

Studies suggest that Mindfulness meditation practitioners have greater empathy and compassion, and feel more satisfied with their personal relationships.
